My Methodology

1. Identify the Bottleneck

Define the operational inefficiency or policy gap through stakeholder interviews and initial system analysis.

2. Data Collection & Structuring

Extract, transform, and load (ETL) quantitative data from disparate sources, ensuring data integrity and demographic precision.

3. Modeling & Visualization

Apply predictive analytics and build intuitive dashboards (Tableau/Power BI/Shiny) to make complex data accessible to non-technical leaders.

4. Implement & Iterate

Deploy the solution, monitor Results-Based Accountability metrics, and continuously refine the workflow based on user feedback.

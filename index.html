About Me

Hello!👋 Welcome to my page. My name is **Nicholas Wiggins**, and I specialize in bridging the gap between raw data, operational efficiency, and actionable public policy. My journey into the world of data began over a decade ago in the Marine Corps, leading me through roles in acquisition and procurement, and ultimately driving my current focus on continuous improvement and data analytics.

With a Master's in Data Analytics and Policy from Johns Hopkins University and an MBA, I've developed a strong foundation in statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and workflow re-engineering. Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work with organizations like the Department of Defense and CISA, where I leverage tools like R, Python, Tableau, and Power Automate to analyze complex datasets, build KPI tracking tools, and re-engineer mission-critical workflows.

When I'm not immersed in data and process optimization, I'm passionate about personal growth. I love to travel, experience new cultures, and constantly seek out opportunities to learn—whether that's mastering a new programming language or exploring a new country.

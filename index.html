About Me

I am a Data Analytics and Continuous Improvement professional specializing in bridging the gap between raw data, operational efficiency, and actionable public policy.

With a Master of Science in Data Analytics and Policy from Johns Hopkins University, an MBA, and a foundational career as an Avionics Technician in the United States Marine Corps, I bring a unique blend of analytical rigor and disciplined execution to every initiative.

Currently, I leverage tools like R, Python, SQL, Tableau, and Power Automate to re-engineer mission-critical workflows and develop predictive models for the Department of Defense. I am deeply passionate about civic tech, quantitative research, and transforming complex demographic datasets into intuitive, interactive dashboards that empower leadership to make strategic, data-driven decisions.
